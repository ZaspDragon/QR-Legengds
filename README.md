QR Legends

Cloud-Based QR Warehouse Management System (WMS)

QR Legends is a lightweight, scalable warehouse management platform designed to simplify inventory tracking, container management, and operational logging using QR codes and cloud data.

The system allows warehouses to generate QR codes for inventory, containers, and deliveries while providing a centralized dashboard for tracking activity across locations.

⸻

Overview

QR Legends is designed for:

• warehouses
• distribution centers
• manufacturing facilities
• logistics operations

Instead of relying on expensive enterprise WMS systems, QR Legends uses:
	•	QR codes
	•	cloud storage
	•	mobile scanning
	•	real-time activity logs

to create a simple but powerful warehouse operating system.
